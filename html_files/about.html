Our story

After 15 years of working around master chefs like Huub Biro, Hans van Wolde and Hans Snijders. Gilbert von Berg became head chef of Chateau Neercanne. The dream to open an own restaurant was always there, however the spot had to be perfect. Small, warm, cozy and most important of all there had to be interaction between guests and the kitchen.
